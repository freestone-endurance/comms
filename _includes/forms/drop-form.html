If an aid station captain, medical personnel, or the runner themselves determines that a runner cannot continue, the runner must turn in their bib and register as a drop by completing this form. If a runner drops, the aid station captain will do their best to help them find a ride back to the start/finish, but their main priority remains captaining the aid station.

Drop Type (circle one):

  • Voluntary Drop
  • Medical / Captain-Instructed Drop
  • Time Cut

Remove the runner's bib and attach it to this form.

Comms / Runner Tracking Instructions

  • Send all drops to CommsHQ via Discord or SMS.
  • If Time Cut: mark the runner as dropped on Inbound time.
  • Otherwise: mark the runner as dropped on Outbound time.
